KWebTest - Compile-HowTo

Introduction:
	-This file explains how to get KWebTest up and running in your machine

Prerequisites
	- You must have KDE development packages installed on your system
	- On Fedora/RHEL these are called "KDE Development"
	- On ubuntu/debian you need "kdesdk" and "qt-devel" in addition to "kde" packages

Compilation procedure
	-cd into the directory
	-"qmake"
	-"make"
	-./KWebTest
